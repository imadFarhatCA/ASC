Design and construction of a specialized medical testing facility during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Executed at the height of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, this project demanded exceptional health and safety measures. The challenge was to design and build a state-of-the-art laboratory environment while enforcing strict protocols to prevent infection and ensure the well-being of all personnel on-site.

We adopted a highly controlled construction process with specialized planning, limited crew rotations, and continuous sanitization procedures. The design integrated advanced ventilation systems and lab-ready finishes to meet strict medical standards. Despite the global uncertainty and logistical challenges of the pandemic, the project was completed on time and to the highest quality expectations.

Highlights

• Design and Build delivery during the COVID-19 pandemic
• Advanced medical laboratory with strict hygiene and safety controls
• On-site infection prevention and controlled working protocols
• Integration of specialized lab infrastructure and finishes
• Successful on-time completion under exceptional global conditions
