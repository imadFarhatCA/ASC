We successfully executed the Al Wafrah Summer House project in Kuwait, delivering the architect’s concept with the highest standards of construction quality and precision. Our scope covered the full implementation of architectural details, finishes, and structural coordination to achieve the project’s distinctive curvilinear design and refined interior spaces.

Every phase—from structural works to mechanical and electrical integration—was carried out with close attention to quality control, ensuring seamless alignment with the design intent. The result is a durable and elegantly crafted summer residence that reflects our commitment to excellence in project execution.
