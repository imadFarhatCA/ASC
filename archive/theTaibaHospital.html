Taiba Hospital Renovation

the TAIBA hospital project

1.

SCOPE OF WORK

Full renovation of all floors in two connected hospital buildings

2.

SECTOR

Health Care

3.

PROJECT SIZE

15,600 Square Meters

Fast-track modernization of an active healthcare facility with zero disruption to patient care.

Taiba Hospital is composed of three connected buildings designed to look and operate as one. Our mission was to renovate all the floors in two of these buildings while the hospital remained fully functional and continued to receive patients daily.

We executed the work as a fast-track project, ensuring rapid delivery without compromising on quality or safety. Every detail was planned to minimize disruption to hospital operations, maintain a smooth flow for patients and staff, and meet strict health and cleanliness standards.

Highlights

• Full renovation of all floors in two active hospital buildings
• Fast-track execution to reduce downtime and disruption
• Careful coordination between hospital teams and construction staff
• Adherence to hospital safety, hygiene, and operational requirements
• Consistent delivery of high-quality finishes on tight deadlines
