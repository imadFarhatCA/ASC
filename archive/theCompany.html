the Company

ART Specialized Construction Inc.

ART Specialized Construction Inc.’s Interior Architectural Design & Construction division delivers both inspired design and uncompromising execution for luxury residences, corporate offices, restaurants, gardens, spas, and hospitals. With projects spanning the Middle East and Canada, we pair an international design language with a steadfast commitment to quality workmanship, ensuring every detail is built to spec.

Our collaborative process begins with in-depth client engagement, understanding your vision, site conditions, budget, and programmatic needs, so that each project’s identity is clear from day one. Comprehensive design documentation and meticulous planning then guide our execution teams, who uphold the highest standards of craftsmanship, adhere strictly to specifications, and keep every milestone on schedule.

This dedication to both creative excellence and delivery discipline is woven into every project we undertake. Backed by a vibrant team of skilled designers and construction professionals, we have earned the trust of leading developers and operators, transforming ambitious concepts into signature destinations that delight on time and within budget.
